About us

Our story, our mission

Getting to know Pragya Convent School — who we are and what we stand for.

Our History

Pragya Convent School was established in 2018 in Kankar, Rewa, with a simple goal — to give children in our community access to quality education in a safe, caring environment. In 2023, the school moved to its current location in Katra, Rewa, where it continues to serve families across the area, offering education from Nursery to Class 8.

Our Mission

We aim to build a strong foundation for every child — academically, socially, and morally. We believe early education shapes a lifetime of learning, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Our Values
